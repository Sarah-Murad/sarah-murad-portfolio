Arisen

A mask company that rises up together

The Challenge

People all over the world have been purchasing masks to keep each other safe when going out in public. The issue with wearing a mask is that it often feels like it furthers the isolation people have already been experiencing with Covid-19. So I took on the task of creating a mask company that didn't only aim to sell, but also aimed to create a community of people that wanted the world to be better again. A community of change.
